🌐 Establishing the Asia-Pacific EMS Network

PERIC 2025 successfully gathered experts from Australia, Japan, Singapore, the Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, and Hong Kong, laying the groundwork for regional cooperation.

🧠 Integrating Academic and Practical Workshops

The conference featured 6 forward-looking forums and 4 highly practical workshops (e.g., prehospital ventilator use, EMS data analysis), receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback.

💡 Recognized Academic Excellence

With leading scholars from Monash University and Meiji University, the conference published 24 peer-reviewed papers, earning strong recognition from the National Science and Technology Council (NSTC).
