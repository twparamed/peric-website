About PERIC 2026

Background & Vision

In the face of global challenges such as extreme weather, emerging infectious diseases, and geopolitical shifts, building "Whole-of-Society Resilience" and integrating "Smart Healthcare Technology" have become critical priorities for national security and public health.

As the frontline of the healthcare system and disaster response, Emergency Medical Services (EMS) rely heavily on systemic resilience and digital integration. PERIC 2026 focuses on the theme "Resilience and Technology in EMS" to address these pressing needs.

Conference Objectives

Promote deep exchange among international EMS systems and forge strategic global alliances.
Explore the application of AI and smart technologies in clinical care, dispatch, and risk management.
Enhance the international perspective and professional competencies of frontline paramedics, physicians, and researchers.
Accelerate the practical implementation of smart EMS research to strengthen national disaster resilience.

Organizers

Co-hosted by the Taiwan Society of Paramedicine (TSP) and the College of Health Technology, National Taipei University of Nursing and Health Sciences (NTUNHS), ensuring the highest standards of academic excellence and practical value.
