About the PERIC

Hosted in Taipei, PERIC 2026 gathers global EMS leaders, medical directors, and frontline responders under the theme "Resilience and Technology in EMS". Featuring a distinguished expert lineup from the USA, Australia, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, and Taiwan, the conference provides an unparalleled platform to exchange groundbreaking research, share cross-border operational strategies, and forge a stronger, borderless prehospital care network.
