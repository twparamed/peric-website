About PERIC 2026

Background & Vision

Emergency Medical Services (EMS) rely heavily on systemic resilience and digital integration. PERIC 2026 focuses on the theme "Resilience and Technology in EMS" to address these pressing needs.

Conference Objectives

Forge strategic global alliances among USA 🇺🇸, Australia 🇦🇺, Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦, Singapore 🇸🇬, and Taiwan 🇹🇼.
Explore the application of AI and smart technologies in clinical care and dispatch.
Enhance international perspectives for frontline paramedics and researchers.
Accelerate the practical implementation of smart EMS research.
