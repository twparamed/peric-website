Venue & Accommodation

PERIC 2026 will be held at the convenient and modern Beitou Resort.

Main Venue

Beitou Resort (2F Training Classroom)

📍 No. 88, Ln. 527, Daye Rd., Beitou Dist., Taipei City

Beitou Resort offers world-class conference facilities with immediate access to the MRT network. It also provides excellent on-site accommodation, making it the perfect location for our international delegates.

Visit Official Website

Getting Here

Take the Tamsui-Xinyi Line (Red Line)

Alight at Fuxinggang Station. Head to the 2nd floor and take Exit 2 (towards Beitou Depot).
Cross the skybridge and follow the signs directing you to "Beitou Resort".

Driving Directions
  • From Central Taipei: Sec. 7 Chengde Rd. → Right on Daye Rd. → Left on Ln. 527.
  • From Beitou: Beitou Rd. → Straight across Daye Rd. into Ln. 527.
  • From Tamsui: Sec. 2 Dadu Rd. → Left on Lide Rd. → Right on Ln. 527.
🅿️ Parking Info
  • Parking operates on a license plate recognition system. Pay via kiosks or EasyCard at the exit gate.
  • Cars & Heavy Motorcycles: Flat rate of NT$ 40 per entry (resets next day). Midnight to 5 AM incurs an extra NT$ 20/hour.
  • Standard Motorcycles: Free of charge currently.
